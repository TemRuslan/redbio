Frequently Asked Questions

Are the bacteria safe?

Yes, Sb. thermosulfidooxidans are natural microorganisms active only within bioreactors.

Minimum volume?

We handle orders starting from 100 units/tons. We work with client material or procure it ourselves.

How long does one cycle take?

A standard bio-leaching cycle takes 7 to 14 days depending on the mineral composition of the sludge.

What is the purity of extracted metals?

Our technology allows achieving a purity level of 99.5% to 99.9% for scandium and yttrium oxides.

Do you offer mobile processing units?

Yes, we provide modular processing stations that can be deployed directly at the red mud storage site.

Is the process economically viable?

The cost of rare earth extraction combined with waste neutralization makes the process highly profitable compared to traditional disposal.
